MedSensei: equitable application advising

The medical school application process is undoubtedly challenging. That leads many pre-meds to pay tens of thousands of dollars for support.

MedSensei was built to level the playing field—offering affordable, personalized guidance for aspiring physicians. All services are completely free.

We want everyone who dreams of medical school to have a fair shot, regardless of socioeconomic status.
